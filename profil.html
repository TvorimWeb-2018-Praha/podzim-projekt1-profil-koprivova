Angelina Jolie

Angelina Jolie is an Academy Award-winning actress and Hollywood icon. As a teenager, Jolie originally dreamed of becoming a funeral director. Instead, she turned to acting and modeling at the age of 14. She often appears on many "most beautiful women" lists.

Her tattoos include the Japanese sign for death, a dragon, and a large black cross.
